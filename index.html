The Analogue Pocket.


A multi-video-game-system portable handheld. A digital audio workstation with a built-in synthesizer and sequencer. A tribute to portable gaming. Out of the box, Pocket is compatible with the 2,780+ Game Boy, Game Boy Color & Game Boy Advance game cartridge library. Pocket works with cartridge adapters for other handheld systems, too. Like Game Gear. Neo Geo Pocket Color. Atari Lynx & more. Completely engineered in *two FPGAs.


  • Built-in Oled display: Create a unique look with customizable animations, view status indicators, and switch profile on the fly

  • Premium sound: Built-in ESS DAC for immersive audio; plus 3.5 mm earphone jack, and a mute button

  • Extensive customization: Remap buttons, change joystick sensitivity, or make other adjustments via Armoury Crate

  • Extensive battery life: Up to 48-hour battery life enables nonstop gaming

Retro Made Modern


Pocket is designed around a first-in-class 3.5”, 615 ppi, LCD. With a 1600x1440 resolution, Pocket has 10x the resolution of an original Game Boy. Yeah. We can hardly believe it, either. Pro level color accuracy, dynamic range, and brightness. The display is even made from Gorilla® Glass. There has never been a display this advanced in a video game system.

Analogue isn’t just for games either. It’s for making music. Pocket has a digital audio workstation built in called Nanoloop. It's a synthesizer and a sequencer. Designed for music creation and live performance. Shape, stretch and morph sounds. Capture music or play and sculpt live.

Compatable


Pocket to MIDI USB-A Cable Pocket to Mac or PC via USB-A and sync Nanoloop to any MIDI-enabled DAW or software

Pocket to MIDI IN Cable Pocket to MIDI and run Nanoloop in sync with external MIDI gear like keyboards, synths & more

Pocket to Analog Sync Cable Sync your Pocket via Analog Sync to additional Pockets or external hardware and instruments

Pocket to Pocket Link Cable Sync two Pockets running Nanoloop via original-style link port
